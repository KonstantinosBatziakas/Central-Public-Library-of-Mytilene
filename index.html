About the Library

Over a Century of
Knowledge

The Public Central Library of Mytilene is the largest library on Lesbos. Its origins trace back to 1913, following the liberation of the island from Ottoman rule, when the local press first called for the creation of a public library open to all. It was formally established as a Municipal Library by decision of the Mytilene City Council, later becoming a public institution in 1952.

Today, the library is housed in the former Doukarou clinic, a historic building in the centre of the city on Smyrnis Street. It offers free access to its rich collections — including rare manuscripts, local archives, and over 110,000 items — to students, researchers, and readers from across the island.

Through its digital repository Ψηφιακός Ηρόδοτος, the library also provides free online access to thousands of pages of rare local documents, books, and archival material from 1620 to the present day.

Library Collections

Explore the
Collections

📜
01

Classical Literature

Κλασική Λογοτεχνία

Ancient Greek texts, philosophical works, and epic poetry — including the rare Vernardakis Collection of ancient Greek author editions.

📚
02

Lesbian Literature

Λεσβιακή Λογοτεχνία

A significant collection of books by Mytilene authors, donated by the Society of Lesbian Studies, alongside works by celebrated local writers like Stratis Myrivilis.

🗺️
03

Historical Archives

Ιστορικά Αρχεία

Rare manuscripts, maps, and the Thielpi Lefkias Archive — a journalist and collaborator of Stratis Myrivilis — covering 1777 to 1979, plus the Syropoulos Civil War archive.

💻
04

Digital Repository

Ψηφιακό Αποθετήριο

Ψηφιακός Ηρόδοτος — free online access to 325,800 pages across 785 rare digitised documents, from 1620 to the present, via the openABEKT platform.

🔬
05

Academic & Scientific

Ακαδημαϊκά & Επιστημονικά

Journals, theses, and research publications supporting students and academics, including catalogues from the University of the Aegean and Mytilene model high school.

👶
06

Children's Section

Παιδικό Τμήμα

A dedicated section for young readers with picture books, educational resources, and reading promotion programmes open to all schools of the Municipality of Mytilene.
